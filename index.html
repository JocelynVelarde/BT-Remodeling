Frequently Asked Questions 🔍

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What is included in a free remodeling estimate?

A free estimate typically includes a detailed written scope of work. Sometimes, renderings are provided to visualize the final look of the remodel​​.

Do I need a design agreement for my remodeling project?

Design agreements are generally used for major renovations like whole-house remodels or additions. For smaller projects, such as kitchen and bathroom remodels, a design agreement is often not required​​.

How long will my home remodeling project take?

The duration varies based on the extent of improvements. Smaller jobs might take a few days to a week, while more complex remodels could take weeks to months​​.

Do I need a building permit to remodel my home?

Most remodeling projects require a permit. A certified remodeler should be able to handle all necessary permits​​.

What are the benefits of commercial remodeling?
Remodeling can enhance the rental value of a property, attract higher-quality tenants, and update it with more efficient or eco-friendly features​​.
How will I stay informed about my commercial remodeling project?

Clients are usually kept updated by a project manager who is involved throughout the entire project​​.
